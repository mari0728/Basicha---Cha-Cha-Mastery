THE BASIC STEPS
IN CHA CHA CHA

BASIC STEPS:

  1. BASIC IN PLACE – 2, 3, 4, & 1 / 2, 3, 4, & 1
  2. CLOSED BASIC – 2, 3, 4, & 1 / 2, 3, 4, & 1
  3. NEW YORK – 2, 3, 4, & 1 / 2, 3, 4, & 1
  4. SPOT TURN – 2, 3, 4, & 1
  5. HAND TO HAND – 2, 3, 4 & 1

BASIC IN PLACE (MALE)

STEP # COUNT STEPS POSITIONS TURN
1 2 CLOSE LF TO RF CLOSED NT
2 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO RF CLOSED NT
3,4,5 4&1 THREE STEPS IN PLACE LRL CLOSED NT
6 2 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO RF CLOSED NT
7 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO LF CLOSED NT
8,9,10 4&1 THREE STEPS IN PLACE RLR CLOSED NT

7 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO LF CLOSED NT

CLOSED BASIC (MALE)

STEP # COUNT STEPS POSITIONS TURN
1 2 LF FORWARD CLOSED NT
2 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO RF CLOSED NT
3,4,5 4&1 SIDE CHASSE' LRL CLOSED NT
6 2 RF BACK CLOSED NT
7 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO LF CLOSED NT
8,9,10 4&1 SIDE CHASSE' RLR CLOSED NT

NEW YORK (MALE)

STEP # COUNT STEPS POSITIONS TURN
1 2 LF FORWARD SIDE BY SIDE POSITION 1/4 TO R
2 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO RF SIDE BY SIDE POSITION 1/8 TO L
3,4,5 4&1 SIDE CHASSE' LRL CLOSED POSITION 1/8 TO L ON COUNT 4
6 2 RF FORWARD SIDE BY SIDE POSITION 1/4 TO L
7 3 TRANSFER WEIGHT TO LF SIDE BY SIDE POSITION 1/8 TO R
8,9,10 4&1 SIDE CHASSE' RLR CLOSED POSITION 1/8 TO R ON COUNT 4

About

The basiCha website is created to support students in mastering the Cha-Cha dance through engaging, interactive, and multimedia-based e-learning materials. It aims to enhance dance education by offering step-by-step video tutorials, animations, and self-paced activities that cater to different learning styles.

This platform serves as a valuable tool for students, teachers and dance instructors for making Physical Education more accessible, fun, and effective. By combining creativity with technology, basiCha encourages learners to build their confidence, rhythm, and dance skills anytime and anywhere, promoting mastery learning in a modern and student-friendly way.
